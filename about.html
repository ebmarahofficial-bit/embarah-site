About Ebmarah

Ebmarah A.K.A. Cody is a fictional character in the EDM artist scene pronounced: ehb-mar-uh. The artist name is actually "Harambe" backwards.

Due to the unfortunate nature of how much Marijuana is consumed by the artist at hand, comedy and hilarity are the two attractive features with obnoxious vocals and gut-wrenching bass design.

Cody at heart is a creative character, with such lines as "in my ass" and "I want to try it out". With many years behind him in the EDM community and top-notch support from the homies, he continues to become a longstanding figure in the race for being cancelled in the electronic drama community.

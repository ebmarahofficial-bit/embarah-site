About Ebmarah

Ebmarah A.K.A. Cody is a fictional character in the EDM artist scene pronounced: ehb-mar-uh. The artist name is actually "Harambe" spelled backwards.

Harambe (May 27, 1999 – May 28, 2016) was a western lowland gorilla who lived at the Cincinnati Zoo from 2014 to 2016.

Due to the unfortunate nature of how much Marijuana is consumed by the artist at hand, comedy and hilarity are the two attractive features with obnoxious vocals and gut-wrenching bass design.

Cody at heart is a creative character, with such lines as "in my ass" and "I’m not here for fun, I’m here for bass".
